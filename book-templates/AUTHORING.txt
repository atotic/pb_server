Page structure:
div.#main-container 
div.page-enclosure -- data page_id, page
	div.book-page  | transform 
		div.book-image | transform
			img.actual-image
  div.book-text

- Pages are rendered onto pdf with webkit. If you use prefixed 
  properties, make sure they are prefixed with -webkit- in your template. 
  Otherwise the pdf will not work.

- do not use any html #id attributes in the template. Multiple versions
  of the template can occupy same html page
